{{/* Explanatory copy */}}

The zero lower bound era (2009–2015) compressed interest rate variance. When rates barely move, they cannot explain much variance — they have almost none. As equity prices became the primary source of market variation, PC1 began loading more heavily on SPX indicators.

The 2018 Q4 selloff and rate hiking cycle added a new pattern of equity-led stress. By fold 23, trained through 2019, the covariance structure had tipped.

An investor using credit spread levels as the primary risk signal was correct in 2022 — BAA10Y widening preceded the bear market. The same signal was uninformative in 2024's bull market. The dominant fear had changed.
