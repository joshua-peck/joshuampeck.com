Short bio

Joshua M. Peck is an investor and technologist focused on quantitative finance and frontier technology. He runs TrueCode Capital, a private investment firm, publishes independent research at Exponential Investing, and wrote Cryptocurrency Risk Management (Rethink Press, 2022).

Long bio

Joshua M. Peck is an investor and technologist who brings quantitative tools to emerging industries. He is the founder and chief investment officer of TrueCode Capital, a private investment management company that applies systematic strategies to markets and frontier technology. He is the author of Cryptocurrency Risk Management (Rethink Press, 2022), a framework for institutional investors that mapped the counterparty risks exposed by the FTX collapse months before it happened. That timing led outlets including CBS, Reuters, and the Wall Street Journal to seek his commentary during the 2022 crypto crisis. He publishes independent research on markets and frontier technology at Exponential Investing, where he writes about where the curves are bending before the consensus catches up.
